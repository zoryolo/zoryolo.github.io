Frequently asked questions

            

              
What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in at 17:00 and check-out at 12:00.

              
Is there a minimum stay?
Yes, the minimum stay is 2 nights.

              
Are pets allowed?
No, pets are not allowed in the apartments.

              
How can I book and how fast do you reply?
By WhatsApp or phone. We usually reply very quickly.

              
How do I get the final price?
We confirm it by WhatsApp or phone based on dates and available apartment.

              
Which apartment is best for me?
We recommend Sol, Luna or Estrella according to guests and trip type.
