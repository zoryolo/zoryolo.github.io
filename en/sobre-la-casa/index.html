Sol, Luna and Estrella apartments

Three different apartment options in the same rural estate, each with its own photo set and layout.

Apartment Sol

Double room + master bedroom, living/dining area, full kitchen, full bathroom and baby cot/highchair.

Rate reference: from €85/night.

Apartment Luna

Double room + master bedroom, living/dining space, equipped kitchen and complete bathroom.

Rate reference: from €100/night.

Apartment Estrella

44 m² ground-floor apartment with one bedroom (150 cm bed), living room with sofa-beds and equipped kitchen.

Rate reference: from €85/night.

Check-in and rules

Check-in from 17:00. Check-out until 12:00. Minimum stay usually 2 nights. Pets are not allowed.
