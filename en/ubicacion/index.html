Route ideas

Simple plans to make the most of your stay

1 day plan

Morning in nature (Toxa waterfall area), local lunch and heritage visit in Carboeiro or Aciveiro.

2 day plan

Day 1 around Silleda. Day 2 in Santiago de Compostela with evening return.

3 day plan

Combine central Galicia highlights: Silleda, Santiago and either Ourense or Pontevedra.

Food-focused route

Try local classics: lacón, empanada and cocido from nearby towns.
