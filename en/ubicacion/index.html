A strategic base to discover Galicia at your own pace

Los Tres Soles is in Silleda (Pontevedra), in a central position to combine nature, heritage, coast and city in one stay.

Why choose this area

It is not just “rural accommodation”. Silleda lets you build full days without long return drives.

Well-connected inland axis: easy access to Santiago, Ourense, Lalín and the Pontevedra area.

High-value nature routes: Toxa waterfall and the Toxa-Carboeiro trail (around 6 km) for landscape-focused plans.

Strong medieval heritage: Carboeiro, Aciveiro and Pazo de Oca are all suitable for half-day visits.

Flexible planning: you can alternate city visits and rural downtime without changing accommodation.
